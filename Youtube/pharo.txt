Pharo (http://pharo.org) is a pure dynamic and reflective object programming language inspired by Smalltalk. Pharo offers a powerful environment with unique features (https://pharo.org/features). It focuses on simplicity (full syntax on a postcard) and immediate feedback. This video is part of the Pharo mooc (http://mooc.pharo.org) and (https://www.fun-mooc.fr/courses/). You can see some success stories at: (http://pharo.org/success). Join the community (https://pharo.org/community). You can find subtitles in French, English, Japanese, Spanish, but also fully dubbed version of the current videos.
